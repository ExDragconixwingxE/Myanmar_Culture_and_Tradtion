About this Website

This is an informational website to promote our country "Myanmar" to be more popular and well-known all over the world. Our country "Myanmar" may not be the best, although it is a magnificent and peaceful country with multitudinous valuable culture and traditions. If you are a Myanmar nation, we hope this website advises you to value your culture and traditions immediately and gives you information to know that Myanmar culture is more unique and valuable than others cultures. If you are a foreigner, we hope this website gives you limitless information about our marvelous country "Myanmar" and makes you go and explore it more.
We may face hard times while making "Myanmar" more popular and well-known all over the world but we are going through it with a smile together!
